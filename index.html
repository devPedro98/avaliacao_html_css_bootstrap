Features

Category Dropdown

Category dropdown plugin is a simple plugin that displays the
parent and the child categories in a dropdown.

Filtering Options

Product filtering is the main way to direct your
customers to browse relevant areas of your store.
Filters allow them to shortlist products based on
their parameters

Mini Cart

Provide visual feedback when the user adds an item to their
cart, and the mini cart does just that. The mini cart is often in the
form of a dropdown or flyout coming from the cart icon.

Quick Product View

Quickview, site users can see product details and
images through an overlay window while on a
product listing page. This way, they can now see
all the details of a product & faster way to check out products.
